week two

The festival's second week of films brought in great crowds to see Putin's Kiss, Girl with Black Balloons, Joschka and Sir Fischer, Position Among the Stars, Alps, El Sicario Room 164, and Mum. Barry Paris from the Post-Gazette described the week of films as giving deeper definition to the festival's theme and to the "symbiosis of directors emotionally tied to their subjects".
