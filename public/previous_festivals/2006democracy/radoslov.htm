Radoslav Spassov
Stolen Eyes

 

Bulgarian cinematographer and director Radoslav Spassov was born in 1943. He graduated from VGIK ( Moscow ) in 1971, with a degree in cinematography. One of Bulgaria 's most distinguished cinematographers, he has shot more than twenty films, working with some of Bulgaria 's best directors, including: Georgi Djulgerov, Eduard Sachariev, Ivan Andonov, Georgi Stoyanov, Kiran Kolarov, Rangel Valchanov and Ludmil Staykov. He debuted as director in 1993, with A Day of Forgiveness. His newest film Stolen Eyes is a Pittsburgh premiere.
