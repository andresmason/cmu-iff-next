What do you get when a Venezuelan economics student abandons his studies for a life in Montreal, Paris, London and Berlin...? A collaborative film effort in Poland, Luxembourg and Germany and a trip to Pittsburgh! Confused? The situation is a little clearer when that former economics student is Franco de Peña, and the collaboration is the film Your Name is Justine.

De Peña's early globetrotting days led him to the Lódz Film School in Poland, the alma mater of Polish film legends such as Kieslowski, Polanski, Wajda and Zanussi. De Peña joined the ranks of graduates in 1997. His films include: Moze to grzech, ze sie modle (Perhaps it's a sin I pray, 1993) - a documentary on a prisoner suffering from AIDS; Szepty wiatru (Wind whispers, 1995) on a friendship of an old Andean man with his donkey; El Porvenir de una Ilusión (Future of an Illusion, 1997) - a poetic description of dreams of modern inhabitants of Havana; Amor en concreto (Love in Urban Jungle, 2003) a sketch of lives of several inhabitants of a modern city. Apart from his film career, Franco de Peña also played the role of a journalist seeking the alleged unknown Argentinean son of Witold Gombrowicz in a provocative para-documentary Letter of Argentina by Grzegorz Pacek. His most recent film Your Name is Justine makes its Pittsburgh premiere as part of the Carnegie Mellon International Film Festival.

De Peña's investigative interviews with police and prostitutes come to life in the character of Mariola, a provincial Polish girl that dreams of a better life. "Ich heisse Mariola. My name is Mariola," she repeats the German phrase in preparation for a holiday in Germany with her new boyfriend. She kisses her sleeping grandmother goodbye and faithfully follows Artur into the night. Their arrival in Berlin makes it painfully clear that Mariola will need more than a German audio tape to survive their "holiday." Two million people are sold worldwide everyday. Two hundred thousand of them are women sold to work in brothels. Fifteen thousand of them are Polish women. Your Name is Justine is a story about one of them.
