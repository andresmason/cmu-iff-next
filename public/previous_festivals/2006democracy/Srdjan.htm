Srdjan Koljevic
 The Red Colored Gray Truck

Born in Sarajevo in 1966. He lives in Belgrade , where he graduated in screenwriting and playwriting at the Academy of Dramatic Arts . He wrote or co-wrote six feature films: Why Have You Left Me (1993); Premeditated Murder (1996, collaborator on the script); The Hornet (1998); Sky Hook (2000); Normal People (2001); Natasha (2001). Receiving four awards for screenwriting, Srdjan Koljevic is one of the most produced and awarded screenwriters in Serbia since his debut in 1993. Red Coloured Grey Truck (2004) is his directorial debut.
