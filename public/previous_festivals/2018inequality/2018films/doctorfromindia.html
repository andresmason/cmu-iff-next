The doctor From India tells the story of Dr. Vasant Lad, the first person to bring Ayurveda, an ancient holistic healthcare system, from India to the West in the late 1970s. After Dr. Lad’s family guru told him he was destined to become an Ayurvedic physician, he fulfilled his destiny by becoming a holistic health pioneer. Because Ayurveda was almost completely unknown when Dr. Lad arrived in the West, he worried that Western medicine would reject this non-Western approach to wellness. Although a Western stigma may still exist around his methods, Dr. Vasant Lad has helped these ancient practices become one of the most prominent alternative health systems in the world today. This meditative, immersive film includes interviewees like Ayurvedic practitioner Deepak Chopra, while detailing Dr. Lad’s life and work.

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Jeremy Frindel is the founder of Substratum Films, and in 2013 released his first feature film, One Track Heart: The Story of Krishna Das. Distributed by Zeitgeist Films, the film screened theatrically across the US, in festivals all over the world, and won the Best Documentary prize at many festivals. His second feature film, The Doctor from India, about the life and work of Dr. Vasant Lad, is currently in release from Zeitgeist Films and Kino Lorber. Raised in Kentucky, based in Brooklyn, he earned a BA in Film Scoring from the Berklee College of Music in 2001 and has been engaged in the many aspects of filmmaking ever since.
