Holiday Special!

Give the gift of Film, Food & Cultural Exchange!

Dear Friends,

Here’s wishing you a joyous holiday season and a Happy New Year filled with new films! In the spirit of giving this season, we are extending a very special offer to you, our faithful supporters.

We’re offering you first dibs on the 2016 Full Access Festival Pass. Treat yourself to this exclusive offer, or give the gift of world cinema to the film aficionado on your list! More than just film screenings, each program offers the chance to engage with international filmmakers and/or local experts, sample a variety of ethnic cuisines, and experience cultural elements like fashion, music and dance.

We’re offering two, pre-season options for the 2016 “Faces of Conflict" International Film Festival:

The Bundled Festival Pass: $35.00 (a $35.00 savings!)

Access to ALL festival screening events from March 17-April 3, 2016 including an invitation to the Opening Night reception.

The Festival Pass: $25.00 (a $25.00 savings!)

Access to ALL festival screening events, excluding Opening Night, from March 17, 2016 to April 3, 2016.

Take advantage of this tremendous offer, and we’ll send you a printable certificate, so you can add the gift of film to your parcels and stockings. Official festival passes will be available for pickup outside screening locations during the festival.

Benefits of a Festival Pass:

  • Brand NEW international films form world-renowned film festivals like Cannes, Venice, Busan, Sundance, and Warsaw.
  • Q&A sessions and in-depth discussions with filmmakers from all around the globe!
  • World-class dance, theater, music, and fashion shows accompanying the film screenings.
  • Authentic cuisine provided by local, ethnically diverse restaurants. Your tastebuds will be culture-shocked!
