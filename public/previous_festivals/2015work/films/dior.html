Dior and I

Directed by Frédéric Tcheng | 2014 | USA/France | 89 min.



"Frédéric Tcheng's Dior and I is a fashion doc with both a sense of history and a feel for the energy of a work in progress." John DeFore, The Hollywood Reporter

Dior and I brings the viewer inside the storied world of the Christian Dior fashion house with a privileged, behind-the-scenes look at the creation of Raf Simons' first haute couture collection as its new artistic director – a true labor of love created by a dedicated group of collaborators.  With only eight weeks to prepare for his important debut with Dior, the tension mounts as these consummate professionals design and cut and sew and alter, seeking definitive statements of what fashion is in 2014, while remaining firmly embedded in the classic Dior traditions of enduring style and quality.

Awards

  • Seattle International Film Festival 2014, Documentary Special Jury Award winner & Documentary Award nominee
  • Tribeca Film Festival 2014, Best Documentary Feature nominee

Director

French-born filmmaker Frédéric Tcheng attended Columbia University's film school in NYC, earning a Masters in Fine Arts in 2007. Documentaries on which he has collaborated are all fashion-oriented, including Valentino: The Last Emperor, and Diana Vreeland: The Eye Has to Travel. He also edits commercials for fashion brands such as Jimmy Choo, Ferragamo, and H&M.
