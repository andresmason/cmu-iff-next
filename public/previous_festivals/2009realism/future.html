Future Tenant, located in the heart of Pittsburgh’s Cultural District, is a project of Carnegie Mellon University's Institute for the Management of Creative Enterprises and is hosted at 819 Penn Avenue by The Pittsburgh Cultural Trust. The art space features alternative exhibitions and performances by emerging artists from the Carnegie Mellon University community and beyond. Future Tenant provides a laboratory setting for artists, arts managers and audiences to explore the limits of the creative expression, presentation and interpretation of various art forms.
