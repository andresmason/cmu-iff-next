Hiawatha Project

Hiawatha Project creates original performances exploring specific social questions through myth, free association, and movement. Named in honor of Hiawatha, the great Native American storyteller and peacemaker, the company strives to connect true stories and disparate communities through impactful and amusing theatrical works. We encourage that you attend their upcoming September 2011 production, "Camino", an original play directly inspired by the true life migration and detention stories of a remarkable young couple, Stephany McMullen and Milton Mejia.
