Competitive Shorts Program

April 10, 5:00 PM at the Kelly-Strayhorn Theater



Reception featuring Indian and Arabic food from East End Food Co-Op.

For the second consecutive year, the film festival will include an International Student Short Film Competition. Open to all undergraduate and graduate students at accredited institutions, its aim is to gather student perspectives on the issues of migration. Selected submissions will be presented. and the three top winners (First Prize: $1000, Second Prize: $500, Third Prize: $250) will be announced.

Presented in conjunction with the CMU Filmmaking Club.
Supported by the Romanian Cultural Institute in New York.
