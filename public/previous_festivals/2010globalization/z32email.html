CMU's "Faces of Globalization" International Film Festival
is proud to present

Z32


Directed by Avi Morgabi

Saturday, April 10 at 7:15 PM
McConomy Auditorium, CMU

An ex-soldier faces the reality of a revenge killing operation. His girlfriend faces the reality that she may love a murderer. Z32 is the dialogue that confronts what has not been confronted between them.

Admission: $7 / $4 Students
Purchase tickets at the door or online at cmu.edu/faces

Featuring a pre-film performance of the film score by Brazilian artist Lilly Abreu and guitarist Eric Susoeff.
A panel discussion with Helen Gerhardt, Barry Fisher, Naftali Kaminski, Eran Toch, and Barry Paris with a reception of hummus, cheeses, French baguettes, and pastries from Allegro Hearth Bakery will follow the film.

Sponsored by Middle East Peace Forum of Pittsburgh and J Street Pittsburgh
