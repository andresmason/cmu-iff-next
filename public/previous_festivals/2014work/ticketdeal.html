The 2015 CMU International Film Festival's Full Access Festival Pass is available for presale! The Festival Pass admits the holder to all festival screening events (March 19, 2015 to April 11, 2015) except Opening Night.

PRICES
Full Access Festival Pass (General): $50.00
Full Access Festival Pass (Students/Seniors 60+): $25.00
Festival Sneak Preview: Song from the Forest: $10.00 General & $5.00 Students/Seniors 60+

- Passes do not include Opening Night
