IN-KIND SUPPORTER

The CMU International Film Festival welcomes the support of local businesses and organizations while bringing this international event to Pittsburgh. In-kind supporters have the opportunity to promote their business at various events through promotional tables at various venues throughout the Festival. Please see demographics and audience attendance for more information.

In Kind Supporters receive:
  • Opportunity to expand your audience through cause-related marketing and brand integration
  • Logo included in festival trailer presented before all featured films
  • Logo on Festival website with link to company’s website
  • Acknowledgement in official printed and digital marketing materials including select program booklets, posters, postcards/mailers, and fliers

All sponsors will receive official CMU International Film Festival T-shirts, festival programs, festival posters, and other festival memorabilia.
Please contact Jolanta Lion at jola@cmu.edu or 412-268-2212 to discuss levels of support. Custom packages also available.
