Tucked away in Pittsburgh's eclectic, diverse and dynamic South Side, is a delightful discovery: the Morning Glory Inn, an 1862 Italianate style Victorian brick townhouse, bed and breakfast. A wonderful blending of old and new, cozy and comfortable, the Inn is in walking distance of fifty restaurants, numerous music venues and shopping choices. For business travelers, Morning Glory is one of the finer Pittsburgh bed and breakfasts, offering wireless internet and SMART Board-equipped meeting facilities.
