The East End Food Co-op is Pittsburgh's only member-owned natural and organic food market, serving the community since 1977, offering the finest in certified organic produce, herbs, bulk foods, vitamins and supplements, cruelty-free health and beauty aids, organic and natural packaged goods and hard to find items for special dietary needs. The award-winning Vegetarian Cafe and Juice Bar offer a daily array of fresh, wholesome hot entreés, soups, salads and vegan dishes.
