The Punk Syndrome is a documentary about Perti Kurikka's Name Day, a punk band currently climbing its way to popularity in Finland. Filmed amidst practice jams, touring, and an upcoming album release, The Punk Syndrome captures the band's passion for honest punk music in an alternative culture of "misfits". Beneath their tough exteriors, we see the band members' vulnerabilities and desire for real social change. This film is a thrilling and graceful look at a band that shows no sign of giving up or backing down.

@BOUT THE DIRECTOR_



Jukka Kärkkäinen has been making documentary films since 2003 and is the co-founder of Mouka Filmi production company. J-P Passi has worked as a cinematographer since 2000. He has worked together with Jukka Kärkkäinen on various projects as a cinematographer, director, and scriptwriter since the establishment of Mouka Filmi.
