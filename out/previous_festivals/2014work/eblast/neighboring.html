Dear Faces,

The 2013 Carnegie Mellon International Film Festival: Faces of Media is excited to collaborate with The Three Rivers Film Festival in presenting the Brazilian film, Neighboring Sounds, Kleber Mendonça Filho's revelatory debut feature! The film will be showcased Saturday, November 3rd at 5pm in Harris Theater AND on Monday, November 5th at 7pm in Melwood Screening Room.

"Kleber Mendonça Filho's Tiger entrant Neighbouring Sounds explores Brazilians’ fear of violence... 'Look at the windows in Holland,' says Brazilian director Kleber Mendonça Filho, in Rotterdam in advance of the IFFR world premiere of his debut feature Neighbouring Sounds. 'They’re huge. But windows in Brazil are tiny – you can't see inside a house because people are protecting themselves.'" Read more.

Additional info regarding tickets, Neighboring Sounds, and the Three Rivers Film Festival in general is available at 3rff.com.

The tickets will sell out; secure your tickets through online purchase!
