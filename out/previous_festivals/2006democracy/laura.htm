Laura Poitras
My Country My Country

 

Laura Poitras studied filmmaking at the San Francisco Art Institute and the New School and dabbled in the culinary world before making films. A current New York resident, Ms. Poitras is director and producer of My Country, My Country (2006), and Flag Wars (2003). She received a Peabody Award for Flag Wars , which was nominated for both an Emmy and an Independent Spirit Award . Her other works include, Oh say can you see ...? (2004) and Exact Fantasy (1995). Ms. Poitras will introduce her film My Country, My Country and lead a follow-up discussion on opening night , November 29 at 7:30pm (South Side Works Cinema) and November 30 at 5:00pm (McConomy Auditorium).
